The Direct Mail Course Catalogue
1970-2017

The Direct Mail Course Catalogue peacefully passed away in his home this winter. He was 47.

For years, marketing local classes was done in the direct mail catalogue. Though costly, he produced tremendous results and was the only marketing tool education providers needed. Though he will be dearly missed by all that knew him, his purpose has been graciously and inexpensively replaced by shrpr.

A private memorial service was already help. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that you list your course offerings on shrpr.
